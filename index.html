Yuzhe Yang, Carlisle

 IEEE, ACM Member

 OPPO

I received the B.S. degree from China University of Mining and Technology, Xuzhou, China, in 2019 and received the M.S. degree from University of Southampton, Southampton, U.K., in 2020. Currently, I am a computer vision algorithm engineer at OPPO. My research interests include multimedia affective computing, multimedia quality assessment, multimodal multitask learning and representation learning.

Live a productive life.
This is not the end. It is not even the beginning of the end. But it is, perhaps, the end of the beginning.
